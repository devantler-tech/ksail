Description:
  Generate a native Kubernetes resource from the service category.

Usage:
  gen native services [command] [options]

Options:
  -?, -h, --help  Show help and usage information

Commands:
  ingress-class  Generate a 'networking.k8s.io/v1/IngressClass' resource.
  ingress        Generate a 'networking.k8s.io/v1/Ingress' resource.
  service        Generate a 'core/v1/Service' resource.
